  • STORE MEMORY ON YOUR OWN 2018 VER.

    Playing Cards, Printed on paper

    The inspiration came from my concern of the problem of ‘transactive memory,’ where people became addictively reliant on technology as a reference of knowledge and not their own brains. I wanted to alert people of their lack of awareness of their surroundings.I once tested this set of playing cards on my friends. They are surprised of how they forgot simple, personal questions such as ‘grandparent’s birthday’ and are guilty and anxious of it.
